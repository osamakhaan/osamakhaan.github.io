Biography

I am a 3rd year Global PhD fellow at New York University advised by Prof. Yi Fang. Prior to this, I received my B.S (summa cum laude) in Electrical Engineering from NYU Abu Dhabi, with an undergraduate thesis on curved lane detection for autonomous driving. My current research interests lie at the intersection of 3D computer vision, medical imaging, and self-supervised learning. Concretely, I develop memory and data (label) efficient algorithms for various 3D computer vision tasks including 3D medical imaging.
